About

This portfolio features the works done by me, Peh Jiawei. A recent graduate from the Diploma of Design Communications, Lasalle College of the Arts. I have been passionate in design and have put my heart into coming up with the best designs within my limit. Besides graphic design, I am also interested in illustration, 3D modelling, and have been actively improving on my photography skills, in hopes that one day my photographs can be recognised.

Though conventional in my designs, I am able to adapt to different situations and come up with the most suitable and desired designs.
